Dan Pradana

A decade in advertising, shaping narratives across TV commercials, digital media, print, and brand storytelling. Worked with top agencies including BBDO, Flock Creative Network, collaborating with brands like Mastercard, Danone, P&G, Kalbe Group, Indofood, HSBC, CIMB Niaga and BCA to craft engaging campaigns and refine brand messaging.

Born and raised in Jakarta, a city where urban chaos meets cultural richness, Dan draws inspiration from its dynamic blend of tradition and modernity. The streets, the people, and the ever-evolving creative landscape fuel his storytelling approach, embedding authenticity into his work.

Now deeply immersed in the world of Generative AI, Dan is exploring where creativity meets technology. His focus is on researching, testing, and integrating AI tools into creative workflows, redefining the next evolution of storytelling—from AI-assisted content production to experimental AI filmmaking. Every tool and algorithm becomes an opportunity to expand creative possibilities.

Beyond professional pursuits, Dan’s passion for fashion, street culture, and Asian pop culture fuels his creative exploration. From the visual dynamism of manga, anime, and video games to the emotional depth of K-dramas and Hong Kong cult cinema, his love for Asia drives the pursuit of storytelling that transcends Hollywood conventions, uncovering narratives yet to be explored.
