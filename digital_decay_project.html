Digital Decay: A Glitch in Humanity

This series explores the fusion of human form and digital entropy, capturing a dystopian aesthetic where the boundaries between flesh and code blur. Created using MidJourney V6.1 with a combination of --sref for stylistic refinement, the images juxtapose sleek futurism with fragmented, glitch-like textures. Reflections, distortions, and pixelated decay evoke themes of identity, transformation, and the fragility of humanity in a digitized world. Each frame is a visual narrative of connection and disintegration, embodying a haunting yet striking vision of the future.
