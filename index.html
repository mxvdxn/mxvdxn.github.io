MXVDXN

Think of it as Alt-Media or Digital Zine Culture rewired for the generative age.

The philosophy here is rooted in the spirit of independent publishing and street culture. It is about the rhythm of humid asphalt, the raw sexiness of the tropics, and a celebration of old-school visuals inspired by vintage erotica and exploitation cinema. The difference is that we use Generative AI to leverage these ideas, not to replace the soul.

MXVDXN is a small independent lab built to explore the dense, chaotic beauty of Southeast Asian subcultures. This region is defined by its visual intensity. It is a collision of hyper-modernity and deep tradition, where 600 million people live in a state of organized chaos.

From the neon lights of Bangkok and the concrete sprawl of Jakarta to the frantic energy of Manila, the polished speed of Singapore, the heritage of Ho Chi Minh, and the urban rhythm of Kuala Lumpur, Southeast Asia offers a sensory overload that you simply cannot find anywhere else in the world.

The goal of the lab is to bridge that gap. We explore the frontiers of new aesthetic ideas that feel regionally grounded yet globally curious. We treat GenAI as a tool to document this "Humid Imagination," ensuring that the specific grit, grain, and heat of Southeast Asia take center stage.

DAN PRADANA

10+ Years in APAC Advertising // Jakarta-Born Copywriter

Dan Pradana is the operator behind the lab. A Jakarta-born copywriter who has worked across the APAC region for over a decade, he knows the rules of the corporate world well enough to know exactly how to break them.

For Dan, Generative AI is a chaos assistant. It is fast, imperfect, and fun to argue with. After spending years polishing headlines for global clients, he now points that obsession toward the raw, unfiltered imagination of the region. He views the "Lizard Brain" as the ultimate creative director. His process sits somewhere between lo-fi visual research, copywriting instinct, and the weirdness of internet culture.
