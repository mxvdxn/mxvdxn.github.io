The Studio

MXVDXN

MXVDXN is an independent culture lab developing original Southeast Asian entertainment IP with AI. The work starts from regional stories, street-level details, genre instincts, and cultural tensions that rarely get space in mainstream entertainment.

Created by Dan Pradana, a Jakarta-based copywriter, creative, and showrunner with more than ten years of APAC advertising experience. MXVDXN is his space for ideas that could not live inside conventional ad work: bolder, stranger, less censored, and more personal.

Partners

Kling AI, Hailuo Minimax, Magnific, Dreamina, Dzine

Building original entertainment IP, powered by AI

MXVDXN uses AI as a development engine for Southeast Asian genre worlds. The goal is to turn raw instincts, cultural observations, and unconventional story ideas into entertainment IP that can be seen, tested, expanded, and developed.

This is not about making content faster for the sake of speed. It is about exploring stories that traditional production would usually ignore, reject, or over-polish. AI makes room for rougher ideas, sharper cultural texture, and entertainment IP that can exist outside the usual gatekeeping system.

Every title here works as both a finished AI series and a living pitch. These worlds can keep growing as AI video, animation, live action, comics, games, or whatever format the idea demands next.
