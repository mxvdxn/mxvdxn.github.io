The Approach

Building Original Entertainment IP, Powered by AI

Everything in the next section is an example of one method: building entertainment IP with strong Southeast Asian roots, executed through generative AI.

AI video technology has reached a point where it can carry cultural nuance. Dialect, vernacular, wardrobe, street texture, the small details that make a story feel local rather than localized. That makes it possible to develop concepts with a genuinely regional identity at a speed and cost that traditional production cannot match at this stage.

Each series stands as a finished AI production in its own right. It also works as a living pitch: a way to present a world, its characters and its tone on screen instead of on paper. Every IP here is open to further development, whether as continued AI production, conventional live-action, or a hybrid of both.

Project 01

Sari & Ploy

FormatAI Action Series
SettingJakarta x Bangkok
Episodes25 Released
StatusOngoing

Sari mastered Pencak Silat on the streets of Jakarta. Ploy came up through underground Muay Thai gyms in Bangkok. Both lost their fathers to criminals. The Garuda-Siam Initiative, a classified joint agency between the Indonesian and Thai governments, recruited them separately and decided they were better together. Their mothers think it's a scholarship program.

Between missions they attend a Jakarta high school, bicker over snacks, take calls from their moms mid-operation, and slowly drain a very tired police chief's budget. The fights are brutal and grounded. Everything around them is not. That gap is the whole series.

Project 02

Codename: Widuri

FormatAI Filmmaking Experiment
SettingSoutheast Asia
Episodes6 Released
StatusReleased

Ratu Sunyi, the Silent Queens, is an all-female assassin syndicate built on one insight: a woman dressed for a ceremony moves through Southeast Asia without suspicion. Trained in Pencak Silat, poison, blades and social infiltration, their contracts are exclusively drug lords, corrupt officials and traffickers who bought their way out of justice.

Widuri is their best. Her real name no longer exists. Each episode is a single self-contained contract. She always wears red. She never leaves a mess.

Project 03

Kala Kanuragan

FormatAI Action Series
SettingMayakarta
Episodes2 Released
StatusOngoing

In Mayakarta, the stress of ten million people is not a metaphor. At breaking point it manifests as Kala, urban demons feeding on the city from within. Kira Ardhani is the only one fighting back on her own terms: self-taught, freelance, treating exorcism like gig work. Her Akik rings are frequency conductors. Her power runs on the city's own atmospheric pressure.

The series lives where Javanese mysticism meets streetwear culture, and it refuses to explain the contradiction away. The sacred and the street were always the same thing. Kira just dressed accordingly.
