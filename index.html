About

Silat Meets Muay Thai, and the Bill Goes to the Government

Sari grew up on the streets of Jakarta and mastered Pencak Silat before she had a formal education. Ploy came up in the back alleys of Bangkok through underground Muay Thai gyms. They share the same wound: fathers taken by criminals, mothers kept in the dark. The Garuda-Siam Initiative, a classified joint agency between the Indonesian and Thai governments, found them separately and decided they were better together. Their mothers think it's a scholarship program.

Between missions they're enrolled in a Jakarta high school, bickering over who finished the snacks, fielding calls from their moms mid-operation, and slowly bankrupting a very tired police chief's operational budget. The fights are brutal and grounded. Everything around them is not. That gap is the whole series.

AI Filmmaking ExperimentRatu Sunyi6 Episodes
About

The Assassin Dresses for the Occasion

Ratu Sunyi, The Silent Queens, runs on one insight: a woman dressed for a ceremony moves through Southeast Asia without suspicion. Founded in the late 1980s by a woman known only as Ibu, never seen, only heard, the syndicate trains in Pencak Silat, poison, bladed weapons, and social infiltration. Their contracts are exclusively drug lords, corrupt officials, and traffickers who bought their way out of justice. Nobody sees them coming because nobody is looking.

Widuri is their best. Her real name no longer exists. Each episode is a single contract, self-contained, taking her across the region. She always wears red. She never leaves a mess.

KANURAGAN

AI Action SeriesSoutheast Asia2 Episodes
About

Mayakarta is the Monster. Kira is the Protector.

In Kala Kanuragan, the stress of Mayakarta is not metaphor. The collective heat and gridlock of ten million people generates a spiritual pressure that, at breaking point, manifests as Kala, urban demons feeding on the city from within. Kira Ardhani is the only one fighting back on her own terms. Self-taught, freelance, treating exorcism like a gig economy job. Her Akik rings are frequency conductors. Her power runs on the city's own atmospheric pressure. Kala Kanuragan lives at the intersection of Javanese mysticism and streetwear hypebeast culture and refuses to explain the contradiction away. The sacred and the street were always the same thing. Kira just dressed accordingly.
